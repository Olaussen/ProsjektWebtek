Our vision

Our vision has always been to give our customers the best food experience as possible. Therefore, 'The Fancy Pickle' is offering local quality meat. All of this is possbile through a deal we have with the local farmers. Still, it's not only the meat that is local. All of our food are, except our special burger. Vegetables, meat, and even our workers have been chosen from the local area. Their job is to give you the best experience when visiting us. Even if you're small, big, skinny, fat, short tall, old or young, we're sure you'll find something you like here. Welcome, you won't regret!
